Hobby

Listening to music is one of my favorite hobbies. It helps me relax and forget about the stresses of the day. Whether I am in a good mood or feeling down, there is always a song that matches how I feel. Music inspires me, gives me energy, and sometimes even helps me focus while studying.

Cooking

I love cooking because it lets me be creative and bring joy to others. One of my favorite dishes is buttermilk chicken, with its crispy coating and creamy sauce. The delicious aroma and seeing others enjoy it make me feel accomplished. It’s a meal that brings comfort and happiness.

Baking

I love making kek batik because it’s simple and delicious. Mixing the chocolate and layering it with biscuits is so satisfying. It’s a dessert that brings joy to my family and friends, and I love seeing them enjoy every bite.
